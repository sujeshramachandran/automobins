About

Automobins App is developed for Insurance Field Executives. Insurance Field Executives can use this app to know the daily assigned claims and can update the claims by capturing the accident/damage pictures and status. Also the accident location will be tracked by GPS. All information is saved into the Salesforce.com. Automobins App is developed in an assumption that the well developed Insurance Management Application is running on Salesforce.com so that Insurance Field Executive can use this app to get daily assigned Claims and can update claims through this app.
